II. Introduction About Kieu Story

Author

Nguyễn Du: (1765 - 1820) His name is To Nhu, nickname is Thanh Hien.

Home town: Tien Dien village, Nghi Xuan district, Ha Tinh province.

Kieu Story

Release: (1814 - 1820) Nguyen Du wrote Tales of Kieu after going to China. It includes 3254 verse written following to the 6,8 type.

Content summary:
Part 1: Meeting and engagement.
Part 2: Variation and wandering.
Part 3: Reunion
